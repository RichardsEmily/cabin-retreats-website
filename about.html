About us

Mission statement

At Rustic Retreats, we're on a mission to create sanctuaries in nature, offering a perfect blend of modern comforts and rustic charm. Our aim is to help people disconnect from their daily hustle and reconnect with the tranquility and beauty of the natural world. We believe in providing experiences that rejuvenate the soul and instill a lasting love for simplicity and nature.

Our journey

Established in 2010, Rustic Retreats was born out of a love for nature and a desire to share its transformative power. Our journey started with a single cabin and a vision to provide an escape, and today, we're proud to offer an array of handcrafted retreats, each with its unique story and character. We've grown, but our commitment to offering a genuine retreat experience remains steadfast.

Our core values

Nature Harmony

We strive to coexist harmoniously with the natural world, ensuring our practices respect and preserve it.

Sustainability

Committed to minimising our environmental footprint, we implement eco-friendly practices to protect our planet.

Authenticity

We believe in providing authentic experiences that highlight the untouched beauty and simplicity of nature.

Meet the team

Our team comprises passionate individuals from various walks of life, all dedicated to curating an exceptional retreat experience for our guests. From operations to guest services, we come together to make your stay truly special.
